Justin Cranshaw

Urban + Social + Ubiquitous Computing

I am a designer, builder and researcher of social, ubiquitous and urban computing systems. My research explores and challenges prevailing notions of hyper-local urban communities in the age of social and ubiquitous computation. I'm a researcher at Microsoft Research FUSE Labs, and a computer science PhD student (ABD) at SCS@CMU.

I'm often inspired by: music, design, architecture, human-computer interaction, people, coffee, machine learning, photography, engineering, and long walks in great cities. When I'm lucky, sometimes this shows through my work.

Contact me at justin@cranshaw.me, or on twitter @compurbanist.
